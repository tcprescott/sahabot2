## Overview

**Live Races** are scheduled racing events that take place in real-time on RaceTime.gg. Unlike standard async races where you submit results at your convenience, live races require all participants to race together at a scheduled time.

### Key Features
- Scheduled at specific times for your tournament pool
- Hosted on RaceTime.gg with real-time racing
- Automatic room creation 15 minutes before start time
- Discord notifications for all race events
- Real-time race tracking and status updates

### Who Can Participate?
Live races are typically open to all active participants in a tournament pool. Your eligibility depends on:
- Being an active participant in the tournament
- Being in the correct pool (if the race is pool-specific)
- Meeting any tournament-specific requirements

## Viewing Live Race Schedule

### From Tournament Page
1. Navigate to your tournament
2. Click the **"Live Races"** tab
3. View all scheduled, in-progress, and completed live races

### Schedule Information
Each scheduled race shows:
- **Date & Time**: When the race starts (displayed in your local timezone)
- **Pool**: Which tournament pool the race is for (if applicable)
- **Status**: Current race state (Scheduled, Room Open, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled)
- **Actions**: Join button (when race room opens)

## Joining a Live Race

### Step 1: Wait for Room to Open
- Race rooms automatically open **15 minutes** before the scheduled start time
- You'll receive a Discord notification when the room opens
- Status changes from "Scheduled" to "Room Open"

### Step 2: Join the Race Room
1. Click the **"Join Race"** button on the scheduled race
2. You'll be redirected to the RaceTime.gg race room
3. Join the race room on RaceTime.gg

### Step 3: Prepare for Race
- Make sure you're ready at least **5 minutes** before start time
- Verify you have the correct seed/permalink
- Check your streaming setup (if required)

### Step 4: Race!
- Race starts at the scheduled time
- Follow normal RaceTime.gg racing procedures
- Complete your race and submit your time

### RaceTime.gg Requirements
- You must have a RaceTime.gg account
- Your account must be linked to your tournament profile
- You should be familiar with RaceTime.gg racing procedures

## FAQ

### How do I know if I'm eligible for a live race?
Check the **Live Races** tab in your tournament. If you see a race listed and it's for your pool, you're eligible.

### What if I can't make the scheduled time?
Contact tournament organizers. They may be able to reschedule or create an alternate race time. Live races require real-time participation, so attendance is mandatory.

### When should I join the RaceTime.gg room?
Join as soon as the room opens (15 minutes before start time). This ensures you're ready when the race begins.

### What if the race is cancelled?
You'll receive a notification with the cancellation reason. No action is needed. Organizers may reschedule if appropriate.

### Do I need to link my RaceTime.gg account?
Yes, you must have a RaceTime.gg account linked to your tournament profile. Check your profile settings to link your account.

### What if I miss the race start?
You may still be able to join if the race room is still open, but you'll start behind other participants. Contact organizers if you have technical issues.

### How are results tracked?
Results are automatically tracked from RaceTime.gg. You don't need to manually submit your time.

### Can I practice the seed before the race?
Check your tournament rules. Some tournaments allow practice, others require blind races.

### What if I have technical issues during the race?
Follow normal RaceTime.gg procedures for technical issues. Contact tournament organizers if you need to forfeit or reschedule.

### How do I enable/disable notifications?
Go to **User Profile > Notification Settings** and toggle the notification types you want to receive.
